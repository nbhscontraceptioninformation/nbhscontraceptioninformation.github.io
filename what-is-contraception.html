What is it?



Contraception is something that many know a cursory amount about, but not in depth. Contraception is a method of preventing Sexually Transmitted Diseases and Infections, while simultaneously preventing one from to have an unwanted pregnancy, which may otherwise result in an unwanted cost and burden. While contraception may have a cost, it pales in comparison to that of an STI or an unwanted pregnancy. Many people complain that a condom may be not as pleasurable as unprotected sex, but the other party is also able to do contraceptive measures, though only condoms, cervical caps and diaphragms are able to prevent STIs, as they provide a full prevention of all bodily fluids, which are able to carry STIs.
